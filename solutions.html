Our Solutions

Our offering includes a full suite of technologies that combine cutting-edge user interfaces with middleware, CDN, and transcoding solutions. For the quick and secure delivery of media content across any device, we developed an end-to-end solution.

Finding a trustworthy companion is not simple. Finding many vendors for various services and goods is also more difficult and time-consuming. Manage

Our middleware technology organizes video material from many sources, secures, monetises, and tailors your offers to generate quick returns on investment using a combination of Capex and Opex models.

Process

With efficiency and ease of use, our system offers live TV and VOD file transcoding for multiscreen and multinetwork scenarios (DVB-T/S/C, IPTV, OTT, and mobile). With both dual live and files management, it is a special offer on the market.

Deliver

Customize

Easy

Robust

Adaptive

Cost Effective
